About GEDA Group

Global Economic Development Alliance (GEDA Group) Ltd is a pan-African investment company with interest in economic development in emerging economies, with a particular focus on developing the strategic sectors of Africa's economy.

Leveraging over 20 years of expertise, GEDA partners with innovators and entrepreneurs across Africa to unlock the continent's vast economic potential and drive sustainable development.

Our Mission

To accelerate economic growth through strategic investments in key sectors that drive sustainable development.

Our Vision

A thriving, self-sufficient Africa powered by local innovation and global partnerships.

Market Environment

Africa's emerging economies continue to drive significant global economic growth, with the continent witnessing 5-6% annual growth over the past decade. As economic conditions improve, Africa presents unprecedented opportunities for strategic investments across key sectors.

Strategic Investment Sectors

GEDA Group focuses on key sectors driving Africa's economic transformation and sustainable development.

💻

Information & Communications Technology

With new cable projects across Africa, increased telecommunications bandwidth is improving connectivity continent-wide. The global ICT market has experienced phenomenal growth, driven by Internet expansion and digital content innovation.

  • Enhanced Internet connectivity across Africa
  • Digital transformation initiatives
  • Telecommunications infrastructure development
  • Innovation in digital platforms and services
💰

Financial Innovation

Africa has the lowest banking penetration globally, creating massive opportunities for financial innovation. Mobile money and inclusive financial services are key to unlocking economic growth across the continent.

  • Mobile money solutions expansion
  • Financial inclusion initiatives
  • Digital banking platforms
  • Innovative payment systems
⚡

Energy Security

Africa must meet medium-term energy requirements while innovating in clean technologies, nuclear energy, and renewables. Smart energy solutions are among our key focus areas for sustainable development.

  • Renewable energy projects
  • Clean coal technologies
  • Smart grid infrastructure
  • Energy storage solutions
🏥

eHealth Innovation

Healthcare innovation is crucial for Africa's prosperity. We develop solutions that improve healthcare delivery through smart health services, combining health systems with ICT in modern, cost-effective ways.

  • Telemedicine platforms
  • Digital health records
  • Medical equipment innovation
  • Healthcare accessibility solutions
🎓

Education Technology

Education catalyzes African growth through localized curricula and skill development. We partner with stakeholders to provide smart education technologies and design localized solutions for institutions.

  • Smart education platforms
  • Localized learning solutions
  • Digital skills training
  • Educational infrastructure development
🌾

AgriTech & Business

Agriculture contributes over 30% of GDP in most African countries and is the continent's largest employer. We revolutionize the sector through technology integration and environmentally sustainable practices.

  • Precision farming technologies
  • Sustainable agricultural practices
  • Supply chain optimization
  • Climate-smart agriculture
